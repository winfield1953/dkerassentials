About Laboratory Hair Studio

Established in 2005, Laboratory Hair Studio is a full service salon located in the heart of Morristown, NJ. Our salon features a collection of highly trained stylists committed to providing exceptional service in a friendly, fun, and comfortable environment. We specialize in precision cuts, curly hair, lived-in hair, and balayage.

Each member of our team participates in a rigorous, ongoing education program to continuously enhance their knowledge and technical skills. This ensures you will experience the most current and emerging trends during your service(s).

Laboratory Hair Studio values passion, innovation, and creativity. We are here to make you look and feel your very best.

Services

Laboratory Hair Studio employs the European Level System of pricing for hair services. Our level system is based on the individual performance, education, and experience of our stylists. We are happy to assist you in determining the best stylist to accommodate your unique style and budget..

Click here to view our full list of services.

Haircut

Our artistic team will create a style that is uniquely yours by incorporating the newest haircutting techniques: textured, razor, curly, men’s grooming, and precision & classic styles.

Color

The magic of color will enhance your natural beauty or make a bold statement. Balayage, single or double process, fantasy color, and highlights will add a healthy shine that leaves a lasting impression.

Styling

A special event, a party, a night out on the town…we will create the mood you are in. Choose an updo, glamorous waves, braids, or voluminous hair while we ensure your event is a fashion success.

Hair Extensions

A transformation to achieve the hair you always dreamed of. A perfect match for your natural hair to add volume and length.

Bridal

For your love story, you need breathtakingly beautiful hair. Imagine romantic waves, a retro updo, or a glamorous braid. Add luxurious makeup and you will be stunning.

Meet Our Team

Our team shares a passion for hairstyling, a dedication to continuously hone our skills, and a commitment to provide an exceptional client experience. We work hard to provide a level of service in our suburban location that is almost impossible to find outside of the best salons in New York City.

What We Specialize In

Our stylists are certified consultants in the following techniques and services:

  • Straight edge razor and scissor cutting techniques
  • DevaCurl: Cutting technique designed for curls, waves, and all natural textures
  • Keratin and Brazilian Smoothing Treatments
  • Balayage Highlighting Technique
